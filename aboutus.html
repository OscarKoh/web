About Gradgala

"Empowering the next generation of innovators by connecting students with academia and industry.

Our Mission

At Gradgala, our mission is to provide students with a powerful platform to showcase their final year projects, receive invaluable feedback, and connect with industry leaders.

We believe in bridging the gap between academia and the professional world, ensuring that every student's hard work is recognized and celebrated.

Our Story

Set up in 2024, Gradgala was born out of a desire to give students a voice. Founded by industry professionals, and passionate technologists, we saw the need for a platform that could help students gain the exposure they deserve.

Gradgala has grown into a comprehensive platform, helping students gain exposure and insights. We've seen many success stories of students landing internships, jobs, and mentorship through Gradgala.
